# DSP-003: Storage and Offline-First

## Objective
Implement offline-first architecture with IndexedDB, Service Worker, and network resilience.

## Scope
- IndexedDB infrastructure with DatabaseManager and MigrationManager
- Storage repositories: IndexedDbStorage, CacheStorage, MediaCache, PlaylistCache
- StorageManager for quota management and LRU eviction
- Service Worker with Background Sync
- Network layer: HttpClient, RetryPolicy, CircuitBreaker, NetworkMonitor

## Deliverables
- src/infrastructure/persistence/*.ts
- src/infrastructure/storage/*.ts
- public/sw.js (Service Worker)
- src/infrastructure/network/*.ts
